# Employee Handbook — SAMPLE

> ⚠️ This is a SAMPLE handbook so the tool has something to answer from.

## Working Hours
Standard hours are 9:00 AM to 5:30 PM, Monday to Friday, with a one-hour lunch.
Core collaboration hours (when everyone should be reachable) are 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM.
Flexible start/end times are fine as long as core hours are covered and your manager agrees.

## Remote & Hybrid Work
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. Tuesdays and Thursdays are
company in-office days for everyone. Fully remote arrangements require director approval.

## Paid Time Off (PTO)
Full-time employees receive 20 days of paid vacation per year, accrued monthly.
Unused PTO of up to 5 days may be carried into the next year; anything beyond that is forfeited.
Request time off at least 2 weeks in advance through your manager.

## Sick Leave
Employees receive 10 paid sick days per year, separate from PTO.
For absences longer than 3 consecutive days, a doctor's note is required.
Notify your manager as early as possible on the day of a sick absence.

## Parental Leave
Birthing parents receive 16 weeks of paid parental leave.
Non-birthing parents receive 8 weeks of paid parental leave.
Leave must be taken within 12 months of the birth or adoption.

## Health Benefits
The company covers 100% of the employee health insurance premium and 50% for dependents.
Benefits begin on the first day of the month after your start date.
Open enrollment is held each November.

## Expenses & Reimbursement
Work-related expenses under $100 can be approved by your manager.
Anything over $100 requires director approval before the purchase.
Submit receipts within 30 days through the expense portal.

## Code of Conduct
We expect everyone to treat colleagues, clients, and partners with respect.
Harassment, discrimination, and retaliation of any kind are not tolerated.

## Raising a Concern
To raise a concern, report misconduct, or discuss a personal or sensitive matter,
contact the HR team directly at hr@company.com or speak with your manager.
All reports are handled confidentially.
